## 2024-03-24 - Parallel stream fast-path optimization
**Learning:** In the `ordered_map` implementation used for parallel processing, streams that are largely in-order suffer unnecessary dictionary allocation overhead when simply appending to a buffer dictionary and immediately popping it.
**Action:** Always implement a fast-path that checks if the incoming item matches the `next_expected` index to yield it immediately, bypassing dictionary buffering. When clearing buffered out-of-order items, `dict.pop(key, sentinel)` is a faster mechanism than a two-step `key in dict` check followed by a `dict.pop(key)`.
